## How to Use This MIQ Bank
- Do 10–20 MIQs/day topic‑wise; mark mistakes in an error log; revisit on Day 3/7/14.
- Mix 70% high‑yield topics (Polity, Economy, Environment, Modern History, Geography, Ethics) with 30% breadth topics (Culture, Science/Tech, Social Issues).

## Recent Trend Insights (2022–2025)
- **Prelims:** More application-based, current affairs-linked, and environment questions.
- **Mains:** Analytical, multi-dimensional, and inter-disciplinary; more case studies in Ethics.
- **PYQ pattern repeats:** Many questions are direct repeats or close variants.

## Scenario Variations

- [Scenario: Beginner + High‑Yield First] Start with Polity, Economy, Environment, Modern History, Geography; attempt 60 MIQs/week; keep a one-pager deck; 1 sectional mock every 2 weeks.
- [Scenario: Working Professional + 2 hours/day] Do 8–10 MIQs on weekdays (rotate 2 subjects), 3×40‑min deep blocks on weekends; focus on Polity/Economy/Environment first.
- [Scenario: Repeater + Weak in Ethics] 40% time to Ethics (case studies/values), 40% to Polity/Economy mixed, 20% to CA/Science; 2 full Paper‑1 mocks/month.

## Checkpoints & Scorecard

- Week 1: One-pager deck ready for 6 core subjects; ≥ 60 MIQs attempted; accuracy ≥ 50%.
- Week 3: 300+ MIQs total; accuracy ≥ 60% in Polity/Economy/Environment; error log > 80 entries categorized.
- Week 6: 600+ MIQs; full Paper‑1 mock score ≥ 100/200; time balance acceptable.
- Final 2 weeks: Variance across mocks ≤ ±5%; focus on weak‑area MIQs and mixed sets.
